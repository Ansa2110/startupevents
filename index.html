About our project

We are the central resource for start-up events dealing with all areas of innovation and technology in Kazakhstan. Our mission is to stimulate the growth and development of the startup community, to invest in connecting investors, entrepreneurs, experts and enthusiasts. We constantly update our event database and see all important startup events to make it easier for our visitors to find and participate in them. Our users can find events that are right for them thanks to filters by cities and categories. Collaboration and exchange are key success factors in individual global startups. That is why we are connected with Startups.kz - to help you stay up to date with all the current events that you feel and participate with us.
